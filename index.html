Who We Are

Ultras Sagesse is a lot more than a fan base as we are a family that has been brought together by basketball and our loyal support of Sagesse. Our members are from various backgrounds, and the one thing that brings us all together is the love that unites us in our commitment to our team.

Our Core Principles

Passion

"Our enthusiasm for Sagesse is unparalleled. We eat, sleep, and breathe basketball, and our commitment is the team's driving force."

Unity

" We stand as one, cheering on our team, come what may. Our concerted power is our chief advantage."

Respect

"We have respect for our team, the fellow fans, the opponents, and the game itself. Sportsmanship is the centerpiece of our activities."

Community

"We support the community that supports us. Our different projects and activities are the ways we want to bring a positive change to the world."

Community

"Community above all. We support our local community and give back."
